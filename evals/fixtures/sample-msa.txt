MUTUAL NON-DISCLOSURE AND SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Agreement is made and entered into between Acme Corporation ("Disclosing Party") and Beta Industries LLC ("Receiving Party") as of the Effective Date.

1. Confidentiality
Each party shall protect the other party's Confidential Information and proprietary information using reasonable care. These confidentiality obligations shall survive for a period of three (3) years following any termination of this Agreement.

2. Services and Payment
The Provider shall perform the services described in each Statement of Work. Fees and payment: undisputed invoices are due within thirty (30) days of receipt.

3. Term and Termination
This Agreement commences on the Effective Date and shall automatically renew for successive one-year terms unless either party provides thirty (30) days prior written notice of non-renewal.

4. Liability
The parties acknowledge that performance under this Agreement may give rise to damages, and each party shall be responsible for damages caused by its own negligence.

5. Governing Law
This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Delaware, and the exclusive venue for any dispute shall lie in the state and federal courts located in Delaware.

6. Intellectual Property
All work product, deliverables, and ownership of intellectual property created under this Agreement shall vest in the Client upon full payment.
